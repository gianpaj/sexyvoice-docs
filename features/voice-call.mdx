Voice Call lets you have live, two-way voice conversations with an AI. Speak naturally and get instant spoken responses.

<Warning>
**Before you start:**
- You need a working **microphone**
- Ensure you have enough **credits** (minimum required to start a call)
</Warning>

## How to Start a Call

<Steps>
  <Step title="Configure your call">
    Set your preferences: language, AI voice, and optionally add custom instructions for how the AI should behave.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start the call">
    Click **Start Call**. Allow microphone access when prompted by your browser.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Have your conversation">
    Speak naturally. The AI will respond in real-time. You'll see a live transcription of the conversation.
  </Step>
  <Step title="End the call">
    Click **Disconnect** when you're done. Credits are calculated based on call duration.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Configuration Options

| Setting | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Language** | Choose from 20 supported languages |
| **Voice** | Select which AI voice responds to you |
| **Temperature** | Adjust creativity (0.6 = focused, 1.2 = creative) |
| **Instructions** | Custom prompt for how the AI should behave |

<Tip>
You can change most settings during a call without disconnecting. Voice changes require a quick reconnection.
</Tip>

## During Your Call

- **Noise filter** — Automatically reduces background noise
- **Mute button** — Toggle your microphone on/off
- **Call timer** — Shows how long you've been connected
- **Live transcription** — See what you and the AI are saying in real-time

## Credits

Voice calls are **billed per minute**. The call timer shows your current duration.

Your remaining credits are displayed at the top of the page and update after you disconnect.
